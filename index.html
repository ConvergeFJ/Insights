Monday June 22, 2013

Converge: Here's our Story.

My name is Calvin Prasad. I’m the Projects & Services Manager here at Converge. Allow me to introduce our company and explain what we do.

Since early 2012, Converge has developed a wide range of software components that helps us implement tailor-made solutions for Fiji businesses.

Today, we are a lean and agile 5-member team, based in Suva.

Our products include Accounting & Finance, Sales Management, Customer Relationship Management, Human Resource Information System, Payroll, Project Management, and a lot more. Yes, we have the whole works!
